About Me

Hello, my name is Christian and I'm an experienced quality assurance engineer from Knoxville, TN living in Cupertino, CA. I am currently an Apple Pay SQE at Apple. In my free time I have been learning HTML, CSS, and JS, and I have previous experience with C++. Some of my hobbies include video games, TV, coffee, and spending time with my pets.
